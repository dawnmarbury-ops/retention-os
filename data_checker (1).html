📖 How to Use This Tool

  1. Check your data: When you open this page, it automatically shows if your positions, history, and settings are saved.
  2. Back up: Click the big green "BACKUP MY DATA" button. A file will download to your computer — keep it somewhere safe!
  3. Restore: If something went wrong, click the blue drop zone above and pick your backup file. Your data will be restored instantly.
  4. After restoring: Go back to Retention OS and refresh the page. Your portfolio will be back.
